More details

Tower Hamlets SASS is a crisis intervention service, which provides advice and support to people aged 16 and over in Tower Hamlets who are currently experiencing domestic abuse. Priority for ongoing support will be given to victims and survivors who are identified as high risk, homeless, or experiencing economic abuse.
