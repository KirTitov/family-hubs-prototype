Connect families to voluntary and community services

This is for people working with children, young people and families in England.

Use this to find voluntary and community services for families. You can also use this to connect families to services you find.

! Warning Contact the police if you think someone is in danger or is being harmed. Do not use this service.

To connect a family to a service, you will need:

  • the family's contact details
  • details about why the family needs help
  • consent from the family for you to give their details to the service
