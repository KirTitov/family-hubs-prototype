Connect children and families to local services, groups and support

This service is for professionals working with children, young people and families in England.

You can find and request support from local organisations, community groups and charities.

! Warning Do not use this service if you believe the child is being harmed or is in immediate danger. Call the police on 999 instead.
You need consent to request support. If the person is under 19, you need consent from a parent or carer.
