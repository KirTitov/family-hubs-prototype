Manage your services in the voluntary and community services directory

This is for organisations that have joined the voluntary and community services directory.

Use this to:

  • add details about services you offer
  • change or delete services you have added
To use this, your local authority must have approved your application to join.

Joining the directory

The directory is for services in England. You can join by contacting your local authority.

The directory helps people working with families to:

  • find out about your services
  • connect you with families who need support - sometimes called a 'referral'
